Introduction

What is the ADF Program?

The Advanced Dominance Fighter program is a fictional air program from the game series Ace Combat, originally under development by the South Belka Munitions Factory, later rebranded into North Osea Gründer Industries.

Which aircraft will be considered in this list?

Only pilotable aircraft that have appeared in the latest installment, Ace Combat 7
