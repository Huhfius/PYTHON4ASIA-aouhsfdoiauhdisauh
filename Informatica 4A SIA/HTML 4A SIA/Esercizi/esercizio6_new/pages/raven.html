ADF-11F Raven

"Those two new drones buzzing around have totally screwed up our plans."

Developed in conjunction between North Osea Gründer Industries and the Erusean Air and Space Administration, the Ravens were superadvanced autonomous drones developed as a larger plan to destabilize both Osea and Erusea during the Lighthouse War

Clearly building up from the older Falken, the Raven employes a much faster-looking configuration. Beyond it's aggressive stance, it is also capable of detaching it's command pod for return of studies and observations in case of emergency

The only Ravens ever seen were the pair Huginn and Muninn, observed at the end of the Lighthouse War, Operation Hush.
