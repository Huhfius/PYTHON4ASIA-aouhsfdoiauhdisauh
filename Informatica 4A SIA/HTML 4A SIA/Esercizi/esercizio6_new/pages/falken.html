ADF-01 Falken

"There's nothing on it in our databases. And there's no way the Rebel Forces could've developed it on their own. Who the hell's behind it?!

The Falken was the continuation of the ADF series. The obvious evolution of the Morgan chassis, the Falken was developed by SBMF's successor, the North Osea Gründer Industries.

While clearly a Stealth desing, the Falkne's main advantage is it's use of the Connection For Flight Interface system, or COFFIN for short.

The Falken first saw action during the Usean Civil War, but only as a drone for NOGI's experimental Zone of Endless Artificial Intelligence, only later recieving a manned variant.
